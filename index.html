Hello there! I am Adam Heaney
a current student at Brandeis University
studying Computer Science.

I am from Long Branch, New Jersey and am currently learning a lot about computer science, exploring various skills and acquiring knowledge in the field.

I was introduced ever so slightly to coding at a young age when I got a glance at what my dad was reading. He would always read out of these thick books maybe thousands of pages long, and I was always so curious about what it was. It turned out it was books about programming.

Since I come from a small school, we didn't have many courses related to computer science, and it wasn't until High School that I took my first courses. After thriving in classes about the basics of computer science, AP Computer Science Principles, and finally AP Computer Science A last year, I found my passion and have carried it to Brandeis since.

So far I am only proficient in Java but am learning Python and looking to extend my knowledge to frontend programming. I'm excited to grow my programming knowledge and Brandeis and to explore my creative ideas at Brandeis.
